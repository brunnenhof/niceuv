# Two scenarios

The game is based on the regional model of the [earth4all](https://earth4all.life/). project. The project is about two possible futures:

**Too Little, Too Late:** This scenario reflects our current path. What if societies continue at a similar pace, setting incremental targets but failing to take strong collective action? Will we cross irreversible tipping points and threaten the foundations of the Earth's stable climate? Will increasing inequality lead to a worsening of social tensions?

**Giant Leap:** This scenario assumes that societies will embark on a new path towards a sustainable world by 2050. What if we fundamentally reshape our economic, energy and food systems so that they work for both people and the planet? Can we avoid the worst impacts of climate change and increase the resilience of our societies to shocks? Will we be able to end extreme poverty and guarantee healthy diets and access to quality education and healthcare for all?

Both descriptions are from the [earth4all](https://earth4all.life/) website, accessed on 12 September 2025
