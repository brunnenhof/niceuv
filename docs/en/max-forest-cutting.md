# Max forest cutting

Forests, especially rain forests, are [cut at high rates](https://www.globalforestwatch.org/dashboards/global/) around the world. Details are [here](https://rainforests.mongabay.com). This is bad for several reasons:

1. Forests store roughly half their weight as CO2, when they are cut, this is released to the atmosphere, making global warming worse.
1. The ground they stand on also contains CO2, when they are cut, this is washed out, ending up in the oceans and the air, both of which is bad. (Bonus point: Do you know why?)
1. They are great storage and retreats for biodiversity, when they are cut, this disappears.
1. Can you think of anything else?

You can reduce or even stop forest cutting with this policy. 0 means no limitation on cutting - things go on as before;  10=10% reduction in the maximum amount that can be cut,  50=50% reduction in max cut, etc. all the way to 90 % reduction which is practically a ban on cutting.
