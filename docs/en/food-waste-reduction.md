# Food waste reduction

[We waste a lot of food](https://www.wri.org/insights/how-much-food-does-the-world-waste). With this policy, you can do something about it! You decide how much the percentage of *normal* waste, which is 30%, is to be reduced. I.e. 100 means, you reduce all the food waste to nothing - no more waste whatsoever! 50 means waste is reduced by 50%,  0 means waste continues as always.
