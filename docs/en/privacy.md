# Privacy

No **personal** data is stored and all **game** data is deleted 90 days after the last login to a game.

The "state" of the game is saved continuously for each individual. So if someone accidentally leaves the game app, he or she can log in again with the correct game-ID and their username and automatically return to the page he or she was on when he or she left. The username can be chosen freely, including fantasy names. **But do make a note of it!** Continuous saving also makes it possible to play the three rounds at different times; even on different days or weeks, see [Rounds](rounds.md). 

The game organiser has a button to display all player IDs in case a player has forgotten what he or she called him or herself when choosing their username.
