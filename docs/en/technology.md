# Technology

You need a good internet connection, because everyone will be connected to the internet at the same time. 

Any modern browser with any device (mobile phone, tablet, laptop, PC) will work, but things look a bit small on mobile phones. 

A projector and screen can be provided for the game organizer, as he or she will be shown the **global** results (the players will only be shown the results for their region and ministry).  The game organizer may want to discuss how individual regional decisions have a global impact.
