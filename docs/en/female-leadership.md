# Female leadership

There are two reasons why equality between genders is good for humans: 

- 1) Survival: [We, homo sapiens, have lived almost 2 million years in egalitarian societies](https://www.rowohlt.de/buch/carel-van-schaik-kai-michel-mensch-sein-9783498003272) (This book is so far available only in German) That's pretty long and a good argument that equality is good for survival. Also, [inequality killed equality](https://www.newscientist.com/article/dn22071-inequality-why-egalitarian-societies-died-out/), and in the process [made us unhappier and sicker](https://equalitytrust.org.uk/the-spirit-level/). 
- 2) Even in the social structures we have lived in in the last few thousand years, equality has a positive effect on economic growth. See the paper [here ](https://www.elibrary.imf.org/display/book/9798400246968/CH011.xml) and [here](https://www.oecd.org/en/publications/gender-equality-and-economic-growth_fb0a0a93-en.html). Both are a bit technical. But you can understand them!
