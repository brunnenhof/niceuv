# What you need to play

You need a game master or mistress, up to 60 players, a room for everyone and a modern browser on a modern device for everyone: mobile phones, tablets, laptops or PCs. 

And of course **time**. As the discussions are face-to-face, you should plan at least one hour each for the introduction, each round and the debrief. This time can be spread over several days, especially if you play this game with school students.
