# Increase owner income tax rate

This policy is an increase in the income tax rate, **but only for the owners - i.e. the rich people!** 

0 means no increase, 10 means an increase by 10 percentage points, 5 by 5 percentage points; the money raised goes to general government revenue. The government can then use this extra money to [invest in new capacity or subsizise peoples' expenses](https://www.economicshelp.org/blog/glossary/difference-between-consumption-and-investment/).
