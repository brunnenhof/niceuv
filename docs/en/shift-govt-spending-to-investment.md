# Shift govt spending to investment

Governments choose how to use their spending: primarily for consumption (eg child support, subsidies for food or energy, etc.) or for public investment (education, health care, infrastructure  etc.) 

This policy shifts spending from consumption to investment. 0 means no shift, 10=10% of consumption shifted to investment, 25=25 % of consumption shifted to investment, etc. You can shift at most 50 % of consumption to investment.
