# Extra taxes paid by the super rich

Extra taxes are those for empowerment: In a different policy you can decide to [empower women](female-leadership.md). The percentage of **extra** taxes paid by owners (owners pay 50% of extra taxes even under [TLTL](two-scenarios.md))  is set here by you. E.g. 90 means owners pay 90% of extra taxes,  70 means owners pay 70% of extra taxes, etc.
