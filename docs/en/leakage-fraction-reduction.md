# Leakage fraction reduction

**Leakage** is a polite word for corruption, stealing, or otherwise channeling money into illegal purposes, like bribing someone. In each society there is probably [some corruption](https://ourworldindata.org/corruption), simply because what is corrupt at the level of society is usually called [lending a helping hand](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=quNEXZ5ysaM) at the level of family and neighborhood. Since this game operates at the level of society and its governments, with you being one the Ministers, you can reduce the level of corruption in your region. 

We start by assuming that 20 cents out of every dollar spent is lost to society and ends up in someone's private hands. You can reduce it. Set the slider to 100 and you have reduced leakage (also known as corruption) to nothing! Everybody in your region is totally honest! Congratulations!! 

You can also leave the slider at 0; this means leakage is **not** reduced at all, it stays at the 20% is was at the beginning. Or, you can set the slider anywhere in between: at 30, you reduce the leakage by 30%, so "only" 14 cents out of every dollar disappears into illegal channels.

The more you reduce the leakage, the more any monies spent are spent on their original intended purposes - also called more bang for the buck, because you reduce the amount of [free-loading](https://jimmyalfonsolicon.substack.com/p/why-contribute-freeloading-is-easier).
