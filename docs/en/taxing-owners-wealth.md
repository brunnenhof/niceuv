# Taxing Owners Wealth

There are some [seriously rich people](https://www.forbes.com/real-time-billionaires/) in this world. And they got there because [they pay less tax than you do](https://wwnorton.co.uk/books/9781324002727-the-triumph-of-injustice!). 

So, one policy in this game is to tax them. You decide how much, anywhere from 0% (= you leave them as rich as they currently are) to 80% (= you take each year 80% of the wealth they have accumuated away, so your government can invest in education, security, roads, ...). Or, as always, anywhere in between: 5%, 12%, 25%, etc...

If you want to dig deeper: look [here](https://www.pik-potsdam.de/en/news/latest-news/how-taxing-the-rich-can-help-everyone), or [here](https://www.fightinequality.org/blog/tax-rich-9-reasons-wealth-tax), or [here](https://taxjustice.net/2024/08/19/wiki-how-to-tax-the-superrich-with-pictures/), or just google [taxing the rich](https://www.startpage.com/sp/search).
