# Cancel debt from public lenders

Like in the [Expand policy space](expand-policy-space.md) policy, money you get from public lenders can be canceled. Also called *forgiven* or *written off*.

The explanation is the same as in [Expand policy space](expand-policy-space.md) policy, except that here you cancel debts from [public lenders](lending-from-public-bodies-lpb.md), rather than private lenders.
