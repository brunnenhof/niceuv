# Invest in Renewables

Rather than use oil, gas or coal to produce our electricty, [you can use wind, solar or other renewables](https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/share-electricity-renewables?time=earliest). With this policy, you decide what percentage you want to reach in the future. (95% is the maximum, because there will always be some electricty that cannot be produced by renewables for technical reasons.)
