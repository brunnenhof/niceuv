# The point of the game

### Big issues of our time

The SDG game creates a space where up to 60 players can explore some of the big issues of our time - inequality, poverty, climate change, equal rights for all, food and agriculture, and energy - in a playful way. The game invites you to create a different future to the one we are currently moving towards. We could indeed create a future with less poverty, less inequality, more social freedom within the limits set by our planet, and more prosperity and happiness for all. For us, our children and our grandchildren.

### Help

It's not easy, but firstly there are clever and committed players in the game and secondly the game supports you at every step with hints, tips, suggestions and questions. And a, hopefully growing, glossary is part of this manual. 

The game is divided into 10 geographical regions, each with 6 roles, see [People](people.md) for details. 

### Discussions and negotiations

The game calculates the conditions in each region from now until the end of the century - which is less then 75 years away! The players intervene three times in the course of events to create a different (game) reality for the future: better, more dignified and happier. With each intervention, the players decide **which measures they want to invest in** and **how much** they want to invest. Against the backdrop of a limited budget which changes dynamically over time. These **discussions and negotiations are the core of the game**. They force players to argue face-to-face in favour of their decisions, weigh up the pros and cons of a particular investment plan and make compromises in order to move forward. 

Good luck and enjoy.
