# Introduce a Carbon tax

Carbon dioxide (CO2) in the atmosphere holds back heat that would otherwise have escaped into deep space. [Here](https://news.climate.columbia.edu/2021/02/25/carbon-dioxide-cause-global-warming/) is a an explantion of the basic physics and chemistry. And even though the effect is not linear, it is true that the less we put into the atmosphere, the less the atmosphere will heat up.

So, one good policy to handle global warming is **not** to put carbon dioxide into the atmosphere in the first place. How do you achieve that? Well, you can put a [tax](https://www.c2es.org/content/carbon-tax-basics/) on emitting CO2, making emissions costlier. This will lead at least some people to think twice about the emissions they generate.

In this policy, 0 means no carbon tax,  25 = 25 $/ton of CO2 emitted etc. up to 100 $ per tonne
