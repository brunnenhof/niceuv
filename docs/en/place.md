# Place

You need a room that is big enough for all the players. The players should be grouped together in their regions. How much space you leave between the regions depends on the size of the room. Whether the groups have tables or not is up to you - it depends a bit on the devices the players are using: Laptops and PCs benefit from tables, see [Technology](technology.md). 

If the weather is good and there is a quiet spot outside, you can also go outside - as long as the internet connection remains stable.  The screen and projector for the game organiser could become a problem outside, see [Technology](technology.md).
