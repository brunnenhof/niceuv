# Pensions to all

To fight [poverty in old age](https://www.bertelsmann-stiftung.de/en/our-projects/kommunen-gestalten-den-demographischen-wandel/project-topics/old-age-poverty) you can introduce pensions for all. 

The size of the pension is expressed as the percent of the [GDP](gdp.md) you want to invest. 0 means you invest nothing and leave things as they are, including leaving old people poor. 5 means you invest 5% of GDP; 10 = 10% of GDP  money is transferred to workers (=the poor) and paid for by owners (=the rich).
