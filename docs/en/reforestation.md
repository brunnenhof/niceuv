# Reforestation

[Shrinking forests is bad](off-balance-sheet-financing.md), but you can plant trees as a policy to work against this. This is called [reforestation](https://www.earthday.org/reforestation-fact-sheet/). 

0 means no reforestation, 1 means you increase the forest area by 1% per year, 3 = you increase the forest area by 3% per year ( in 10 years you have increase the forest land by about **34%** (!) - as long as there is land that can be reforested!)
