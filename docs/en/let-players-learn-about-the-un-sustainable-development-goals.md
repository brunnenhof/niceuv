# Let players learn about the UN Sustainable Development Goals

In many schools and other settings one needs to teach about the [Sustainable Development Goals](https://sdgs.un.org/goals), promulgated by the UN a few years back. Like most things done by international organizations, this has become a [monstrous](https://www.vocabulary.com/dictionary/monstrous) [edifice](https://www.vocabulary.com/dictionary/edifice). Time, perhaps, to approach this important topic more *playfully*. 

The goals are the background to this game, and your task is to move your region into this sustainable territory.

There are other games that address the same topic, have a look at [Sustain2030](https://sustain2030.de/en/), or [Das Parlament der Zukunft](https://www.geo.lmu.de/elmundo/de/projekte/earth4all-parlament-der-zukunft/), ...
