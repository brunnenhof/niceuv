    Change diets

    More than two thirds of all agricultural land is used to feed livestock, cows, pigs, goats, chicken, turkey, etc. If we ate less meat, especially red meat, we would need much less agricultural land, or we could use the land we have much less intensively - the land could regenerate more naturally (even Champion League players need time for regeneration!).

    With this policy, you can change the diet, esp. a reduction in red meat consumption. 0 means red meat is consumed as before, 50 means 50% of meat consumption is reduced or replaced with cell-base meat, 100 means 100% of meat consumption is reduced or replaced with cell-base meat, i.e. no more meat is produced by intensive livestock farming aka factory farming.
